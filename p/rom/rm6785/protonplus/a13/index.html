Instructions

  • If you are coming form any ROM based realmeUI-1.0 (cleanflash)

    • Format Data
    • Flash ROM & Flash Gapps (Optional)
    • Flash custom recovery based realmeUI-2.0
    • Reboot your device once the installation is done
